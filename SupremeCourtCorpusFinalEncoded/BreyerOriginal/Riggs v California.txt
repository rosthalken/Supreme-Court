
        
        Riggs v. California, 525 U.S. 1114, 119 S. Ct. 890 (1999)
        


      

I agree with JUSTICE STEVENS that this petition for certiorari raises a serious question concerning the application of a "three-strikes" law to what is in essence a petty [****6]  offense. I believe it appropriate to review that question in this case and would grant the writ of certiorari.
