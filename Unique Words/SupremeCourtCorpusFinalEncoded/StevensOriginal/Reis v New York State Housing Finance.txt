No. 89-5700

SUPREME COURT OF THE UNITED STATES

493 U.S. 931

October 30, 1989

Justice Brennan, Justice Marshall, Justice Blackmun and Justice Stevens, dissenting: For the reasons expressed in Brown v. Herald Co., Inc., 464 U.S. 928 (1983), we would deny the petition for a writ of certiorari without  [*932]  reaching the merits of the motion to proceed in forma pauperis.
