
ORDER

UPON CONSIDERATION of the application of counsel for the applicant,

IT IS ORDERED that the deportation order and the execution and enforcement of the judgment of the United States Court of Appeals for the Sixth Circuit, case No. 86-1504, be, and the same is hereby, recalled and stayed pending receipt of a response to the application from the Solicitor General of the United States due on or before noon, Tuesday, November 10, 1987, and further order of the undersigned or of the Court.
