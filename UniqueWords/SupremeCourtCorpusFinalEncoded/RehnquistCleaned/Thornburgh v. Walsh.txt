ORDER
UPON CONSIDERATION of the application of the Acting Solicitor General and the response thereto filed by the Independent Counsel,
IT IS ORDERED that the trial proceedings in United States v. Oliver L. North, D.D.C., No. 88-0080-02, scheduled to begin on Monday, February 13, 1989, be stayed pending consideration by the full Court at its Conference on Friday, February 17, 1989, of the application of Dick Thornburgh, Attorney General of the United States, for a stay pending appeal.
IT IS FURTHER ORDERED that this stay shall continue pending further order of the Court or of the undersigned.
