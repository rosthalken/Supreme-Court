Justice Brennan, Justice Marshall and Justice Stevens, dissenting: For the reasons expressed in Brown v. Herald Co., Inc., 464 U. S. 928 (1983),  [*44]  we would deny the petition for writ of certiorari without reaching the merits of the motions to proceed in forma pauperis.
