 **2382 Justice SCALIA, with whom THE CHIEF JUSTICE and Justice KENNEDY join, dissenting.

For the reasons stated in my opinion in Chisom v. Roemer, 501 U.S. 380, 404, 111 S.Ct. 2354, 2369, 115 L.Ed.2d 348, I would not apply § 2 of the Voting Rights Act of 1965 to vote dilution claims in judicial elections, and would therefore affirm the judgment below.
