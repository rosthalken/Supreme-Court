1992 U.S. LEXIS 2599
HAROLD JOE LANE Petitioner, v. TEXAS 
Supreme Court of the United States
April 22, 1992, Decided
No. A-771 (91-7678)

ORDER

UPON CONSIDERATION of the application of counsel for the petitioner,

IT IS ORDERED that the execution and enforcement of the sentence of death imposed upon the petitioner and scheduled for April 24, 1992, is hereby stayed pending the disposition by this Court of the petition for a writ of certiorari. Should the petition for a writ of certiorari be denied, this stay is to terminate automatically. In the event the petition for a writ of certiorari is granted, this stay is to continue pending the issuance of the mandate of this Court.

Antonin Scalia

Associate Justice of the Supreme

Court of the United States

Dated this 22nd

day of April, 1992
