
        
        Levin v. Commerce Energy, Inc., 560 U.S. 413, 130 S. Ct. 2323 (2010)
        


      

Justice Kennedy, concurring.

The Court's rationale in Hibbs v. Winn, 542 U.S. 88, 124 S. Ct. 2276, 159 L. Ed. 2d 172 (2004), seems to me still doubtful. Nothing in the Court's opinion today expands Hibbs' holding further, however, and on that understanding I join the opinion of the Court.
