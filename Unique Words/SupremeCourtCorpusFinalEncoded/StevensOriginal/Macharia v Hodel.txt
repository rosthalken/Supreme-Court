No. 88-5808

SUPREME COURT OF THE UNITED STATES

488 U.S. 992

December 12, 1988

Justice Marshall, Justice Blackmun and Justice Stevens, dissenting: For the reasons expressed in Brown v. Herald Co., Inc., 464 U.S. 928 (1983), we would deny the petition for a writ of certiorari without reaching the merits of the motion to proceed in forma pauperis. Justice Brennan took no part in the consideration or decision of this motion.
