No. 88-5580

SUPREME COURT OF THE UNITED STATES

Justice Brennan, Justice Marshall, Justice Blackmun and Justice Stevens, dissenting: For the reasons expressed in Brown v. Herald Co., Inc., 464 U.S. 928 (1983), we would dismiss the appeal without reaching the merits of the motion to proceed in forma pauperis.
