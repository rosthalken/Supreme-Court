JUSTICE STEVENS, respecting the denial of certiorari.
It is important to emphasize that the denial of the petition for writ of certiorari is not a ruling on any of the unsettled and important questions of law presented in the petition. See Singleton v. Commissioner, 439 U.S. 940, 942 (1978) (STEVENS, J. respecting denial of certiorari).
