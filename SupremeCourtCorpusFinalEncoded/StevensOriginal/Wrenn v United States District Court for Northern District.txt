No. 88-6131

SUPREME COURT OF THE UNITED STATES

489 U.S. 1008

February 21, 1989

Brennan, Justice Marshall and Justice Stevens dissenting: For the reasons expressed in Brown v. Herald Co., Inc., 464 U.S. 928 (1983), we would deny the petition for a writ of certiorari [*1009]  without reaching the merits of the motion to proceed in forma pauperis.
