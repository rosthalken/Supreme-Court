AMSDEN, HENRY H. v. MORAN, THOMAS F., ET AL.

No. 90-5536

SUPREME COURT OF THE UNITED STATES

498 U.S. 936

October 29, 1990

Justice Marshall and Justice Stevens, dissenting: For the reasons expressed in Brown v. Herald Co., Inc., 464 U.S. 928 (1983), we would deny the petition for a writ of certiorari without reaching the merits of the motion to proceed in forma pauperis. Justice Souter took no part in the consideration or decision of this motion.
