91-6057

SUPREME COURT OF THE UNITED STATES

502 U.S. 979

December 2, 1991, Decided 

Justice Stevens, dissenting: For the reasons expressed in Brown v. Herald Co., Inc., 464 U.S. 928 (1983), I would deny the petition for a writ of certiorari without reaching the merits of the motion to proceed in forma pauperis.
