SUPREME COURT OF THE UNITED STATES

Justice Brennan, Justice Marshall and Justice Stevens, dissenting: For the reasons expressed in Brown v. Herald Co., Inc., 464 U.S. 928, we would deny the petition for a writ of certiorari without reaching the merits of the motion to proceed in forma pauperis.
